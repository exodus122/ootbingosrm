OoT SRM Bingo

This is a "Bingo" board for Ocarina of Time races.

To win, you must complete 5 of the tasks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The seed number is used to generate the board. Changing the seed will make a new board.

You can click on the squares to turn them green and red. This may help you organize your route planning.

You may need to disable Adblock for the popup rows to work properly

For OoT SRM Bingo, there are some specific rules in place:

  • RBA and SRM are allowed, but ACE and Light Node SRM are banned.
  • Allowed consoles are VC, N64, and emulator on NTSC versions 1.0, 1.1, and 1.2 of the game
  • You bingo filename must be "ラ97ぉ", the one used for Title File in Kokiri Forest
  • If the goal is an item, and does not specify a source, there is no source requirement. You must have all item goals in your inventory at the time you finish getting all 5 objectives.
  • If the goal says to perform an action, setting the flag for that action to be completed (with SRM, RBA, or other methods) does not count as completing the goal. This applies to "Open chest" goals, "Defeat enemy" goals, and all other action goals.
  • Skulltula goals are not counted as completed if you set the flags for them with SRM, RBA, or other methods. You must collect them.
  • You must open the normal chest which contains the listed item. For example, if a goal says "Open Fairy Slingshot chest", you must open the normal chest in Deku Tree which contains the fairy slingshot.
  • If a goal says to Learn a song, you must learn it from its normal source. Obtaining the song with SRM, RBA, or other methods does not count for the goal.
  • Sometimes there is a question of when to say you are finished. For dungeons, you are "finished" with the dungeon when you step into the blue warp after defeating the boss.
